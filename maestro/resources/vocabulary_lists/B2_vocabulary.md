# B2 Vocabulary List - Upper Intermediate Level (2000+ Words)

**Building on A1-A2-B1**: This list assumes mastery of A1 (~500 words), A2 (~1200 words), and B1 (~1500 words) vocabulary and adds essential B2 vocabulary for upper-intermediate fluent communication.

**Total B2 new words**: ~2000 words
**Cumulative**: ~5200 words (A1 + A2 + B1 + B2)

**Key B2 Features**: Idiomatic expressions, sophisticated vocabulary, nuanced meanings, register awareness, complex argumentation, cultural references, implicit meanings

## Idiomatic Expressions & Colloquialisms

- No pegar ojo (not to sleep a wink)
