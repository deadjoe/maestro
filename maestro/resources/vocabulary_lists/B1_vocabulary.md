# B1 Vocabulary List - Intermediate Level (1500+ Words)

**Building on A1-A2**: This list assumes mastery of A1 (~500 words) and A2 (~1200 words) vocabulary and adds essential B1 vocabulary for intermediate-level communication.

**Total B1 new words**: ~1500 words
**Cumulative**: ~3200 words (A1 + A2 + B1)

**Key B1 Features**: Abstract concepts, subjunctive mood, conditional tense, expressing opinions, hypotheticals, complex descriptions

## Subjunctive Mood Expressions

### Expressing Wishes & Desires (+ Subjunctive)
- Esperar que (to hope that), Desear que (to wish that)
- Querer que (to want that), Preferir que (to prefer that)
- Ojalá (que) (I hope/wish that), Que + subjunctive (May...)
- Me gustaría que (I would like that)

### Expressing Doubt & Uncertainty (+ Subjunctive)
- Dudar que (to doubt that), No creer que (not to believe that)
- No pensar que (not to think that), No estar seguro/a de que (not to be sure that)
- Es posible que (it's possible that), Es probable que (it's probable that)
- Puede que (it may be that), Quizás/Tal vez (perhaps/maybe)
- Es dudoso que (it's doubtful that)

### Expressing Emotions (+ Subjunctive)
- Me alegra que (I'm glad that), Me encanta que (I love that)
- Me molesta que (it bothers me that), Me preocupa que (it worries me that)
- Me sorprende que (it surprises me that), Temo que (I fear that)
- Siento que (I'm sorry that), Es una pena que (it's a shame that)
- Es fantástico/terrible/horrible que (it's fantastic/terrible/horrible that)

### Giving Advice & Recommendations (+ Subjunctive)
- Aconsejar que (to advise that), Recomendar que (to recommend that)
- Sugerir que (to suggest that), Es importante que (it's important that)
- Es necesario que (it's necessary that), Es mejor que (it's better that)
- Es fundamental que (it's fundamental that), Es esencial que (it's essential that)

### Impersonal Expressions (+ Subjunctive)
- Es imposible que (it's impossible that), Es difícil que (it's difficult that)
- Es raro que (it's strange that), Es increíble que (it's incredible that)
- Es bueno/malo que (it's good/bad that), Es lógico que (it's logical that)
- No es verdad que (it's not true that), No es cierto que (it's not certain that)
