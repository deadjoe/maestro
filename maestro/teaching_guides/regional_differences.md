**Primary focus**: Spain (Castilian Spanish) vs Mexico

## 7. Common Student Questions

**Q: Which Spanish should I learn?**
A: Learn standard Spanish (based on Spain grammar), but be aware of Mexican/Latin American vocabulary since it's more widely used. You'll understand all variants.

**Q: Is my Spanish "wrong" if I use vosotros?**
A: No! It's correct, just regional. Spanish speakers from different regions understand each other perfectly.

**Q: Should I try to imitate the "th" sound?**
A: Only if you want to! Both pronunciations are correct. Most learners find seseo (s sound) easier.

**Q: What if I learn Spain Spanish but travel to Mexico?**
A: You'll be understood perfectly! Just switch a few vocabulary words (ordenadorâ†’computadora, etc.).

**Q: Is Mexican Spanish easier than Spain Spanish?**
A: Subjective, but many say yes because: no vosotros forms, no th sound, slower pronunciation. But Spain has simpler diminutive usage.

**Q: Which accent is "better" or "more proper"?**
A: Neither! All regional variants are equally valid. Like British vs American English.
